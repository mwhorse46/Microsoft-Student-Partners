MSP Summer Of Code (MSPSoC) is a 2 month long Open Source program with an aim to help beginners get started with Open Source Development while creating solutions for a better tomorrow. Throughout the program, participants can contribute to different projects under the guidance and support of Mentors. Top performers from each project will be felicitated with a certificate, goodies and much more.
